Why not just start your own blog?

Starting a blog can be relatively straight forward. You need to create a website and then produce content and promote the blog. Here are some of the limitations you need to think about:

  • Off the shelf blog themes will not be designed around the specific objective of targeting your intended market
  • Promoting a blog and driving traffic is involved and will take a lot of time and effort. You will get readers, but probably not the high numbers needed for it to benefit you and result in leads
  • You will have to produce a lot of content to keep your users engaged. To generate the amount of content needed to keep users engaged will make blogging your full time job rather than focusing on your core business of selling travel
Why search engines will rank us higher

One the most important contributing factors to the number of people that will read your blog is how high the search engines feature your content. We know that our platform will feature your content much higher than your own blog would because:

  • We have invested heavily in search engine optimisation because we are promoting the content of over 1,000 contributors to our platforms
  • Search engines likes sites that generate new and unique content, but much more importantly, sites that produce content regularly and that can be relied upon to produce content frequently.
  • With the number of contributors to our platform, search engines will identify the site as a reliable source of content and will rank relevant content on our platform higher than they would if the same content was on a less prominent platform.
Benefit from traffic generated by content from other experts

Starting a blog can be relatively straight forward. You need to create a website and then produce content and promote the blog. Here are some of the limitations you need to think about:

  • Sometimes, a user will search for content that encompasses both content featured on one of your submissions and that contained within other content within the platform. The search engine will promote your entry because it will see other relevant content on the site
  • People reading your article on the platform will see other suggested articles at the end of the page which they may click on if the title triggers their interest
  • If a user follows the site because of content from another expert that interested them, your content will then be promoted to them as well

HOW WE BENEFIT FROM THIS

You may be wondering what’s in it for us? After all, this is a lot of work for Major Travel with no obvious monetisation. Here are our 3 main reasons:

We are always looking for new ways to support our travel agents

We do a lot that helps our agents with no obvious return. Our objective here is to help agents promote themselves so that they can generate more business. We know that ultimately the agent decides who that business goes to, and some of it may end up going to our competitors, but ultimately, we have seen that loyalty pays off more often than not. When we help agents, we see that they will return that support.

Negotiating better deals with supplier

With the number of agents and industry experts that will be contributing content onto this platform and with the number of users and followers we expect to have, we will be able to go to airlines, hotels and other suppliers that work with us and negotiate better deals for our agents to sell. Suppliers are often looking to promote exclusive offers to specific operators if they feel they have a distribution network that will help them achieve their target yields.

Bringing on new agents

Contributions on this platform will only be open to employees of organisations that have an active account with Major Travel. We know this platform will be extremely popular and a lot of people will want to sign up to contribute on it.

This platform will be a motivation for agents, tour operators and other industry employees whose employers don’t currently work with Major Travel to encourage their companies to sign up and start actively buying from us.
